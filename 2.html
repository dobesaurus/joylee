Joy Lee, musician

Joy performs on the harpsichord, piano, and other keyboard instruments, such as clavichord and organetto. Her repertoire ranges from Baroque to contemporary.

Living on Wadawurrung Country in regional Victoria, Joy has worked both solo and in ensembles, including Astra, Harken Well, Arcko, Homophonic and Inland.

To hear Joy playing, please visit her YouTube channel.
