Joy Lee — harpsichord & piano

Joy is a harpsichordist and pianist based in Lara, Victoria. She plays both new and old music and collaborates with Australian and international composers and other musicians.

Joy performs solo and in varied chamber contexts. She has performed with the Astra Chamber Music Society, the Arcko Symphonic Ensemble, Homophonic!, the INLAND performance series and is a member of the Six Degrees Ensemble. She is a founding member of Prog-Baroque quartet Croissants & Whiskey, an ensemble giving new energy to old instruments.
