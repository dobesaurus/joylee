Joy Lee, musician

Joy performs with the harpsichord, piano, and other keyboard instruments, such as clavichord and organetto. Her repertoire spans many eras, from Baroque to contemporary.

Joy plays both solo and in ensembles, including Astra, Harken Well, Arcko, Homophonic and Inland. She lives on Wadawurrung Country in regional Victoria.
